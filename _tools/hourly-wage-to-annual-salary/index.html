Annual Salary Calculation

To convert your hourly wage to an annual salary, we use the formula:

Annual Salary = Hourly Wage × Hours per Week × Weeks Worked per Year

This calculation assumes a constant work schedule and does not account for overtime, bonuses, or other forms of compensation.
