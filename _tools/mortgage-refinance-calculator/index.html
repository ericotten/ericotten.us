Refinance Savings Formula

To estimate your savings from refinancing, we calculate the difference between your current monthly payment and the potential new monthly payment. The formula for each monthly payment is:

M = P[r(1+r)^n] / [(1+r)^n - 1]

  • M is the total monthly mortgage payment.
  • P is the principal loan amount.
  • r is the monthly interest rate (annual interest rate divided by 12).
  • n is the number of payments (loan term in years multiplied by 12).

We then subtract the new potential payment from your current payment to find your monthly savings. >Note, this does not account for refinancing costs, which should be considered in your overall savings analysis.
